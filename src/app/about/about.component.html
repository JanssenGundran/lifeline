Lifeline Pampanga

Connecting Communities with Opportunities

Who We Are

Lifeline Pampanga is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping communities in Pampanga by providing an easy and accessible platform for job seekers and employers.

Our Advocacy

We aim to empower individuals by bridging the gap between job opportunities and those who need them most. Our platform ensures a seamless experience for job applications and hiring.

How It Works

Our platform allows users to view, and apply for jobs effortlessly.

1

Go to the Jobs page to see available listings.

2

Click "APPLY NOW" on your desired job.

3

Enter your Name and Email, then submit.

4

Your name will be listed under Pending.

5

Wait for an email update on your status for further Requirements

6

If accepted, your name moves to Approved.
